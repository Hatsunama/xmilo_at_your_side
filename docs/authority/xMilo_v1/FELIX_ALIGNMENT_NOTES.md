# Felix Alignment Notes

This file records what was intentionally imported from Felix-style assistant builds and what was intentionally excluded.

## Imported
- layered persistent memory
- long-running assistant continuity
- simple control structure until a real bottleneck appears
- durable operational identity

## Excluded
- business operator framing
- money-making goals
- ecommerce / customer support logic
- recruiting / lead generation
- market / trading / scoring systems

## Rule
Felix is used here as an implementation-pattern reference, not as a persona template.
Milo remains a personal assistant and sidekick.
